# Money model

- **Free:** log up to ~10 test tiles. Recipes and firings included.
- **One-time $9.99 "Unlock everything"** (NOT a subscription): unlimited test tiles,
  the AI "diagnose & suggest next" helper (includes ~100 AI suggestions), charts &
  insights, line/triaxial blend helper, full export.
- **Top-up:** ~$1.99 for ~100 more AI suggestions (covers the Anthropic cost for heavy
  users).
- **Tip jar:** "Buy me a coffee" in Settings + a one-time dismissible prompt after a
  happy moment (e.g. marking a test a 5-star keeper).

Why this works: logging, charts, and export cost almost nothing to run, so a one-time
unlock is safe. The AI helper costs a little per suggestion (the Anthropic bill), so the
included suggestions + cheap top-up keep heavy AI users from costing more than they paid.

Pricing note: $9.99 matches the pottery market (the nearest competitor sells lifetime at
$9.99) and the "buy once, no subscription, your data is yours" framing is a direct answer
to the subscription fatigue and lock-in fear in this market.

Be honest about earnings — it is a small amount of money. Success here depends far more on
reaching the right potters than on the price.
